FAQs

1. What is the format of the tournament?

The tournament will follow a round-robin format during the group stages, where each team plays against all other teams in their group. The top teams from each group will advance to the knockout rounds, culminating in a grand final.

2. What happens if it rains on match day?

In case of rain, we have reserved backup days for crucial matches. For group-stage games, the match may be shortened (Duckworth-Lewis method) or declared a draw depending on the situation. Updates will be communicated via our website and social media channels.

3.What are the prizes for the winners?

The winning team will receive a trophy, medals, and a cash prize of 20,000/-. Additionally, awards for Best Batsman, Best Bowler, and Player of the Tournament will be presented during the closing ceremony.

4. Is there a fee to participate?

Participation fees vary for each tournament. Check the tournament details for exact fees.

5. Can I edit my tournament details after submission?

Currently, tournament details cannot be edited after submission. Please contact support for assistance.

6. Are there any sponsorship or logo placement restrictions on uniforms?

Yes, the logos of all our sponsors for the respective tournamnets will be printed on the uniforms.

7. Is StumpsConnect available globally?

Yes, StumpsConnect is accessible worldwide for cricket enthusiasts and organizers.
